Infection in the epidural space may be caused by phlegmon or abscess. An abscess is
centrally necrotic, although either may exert mass effect upon the nerve roots or spinal
cord.
Given that normal epidural fat is hyperintense on T1-weighted images, fat suppression is an
extremely helpful technique to detect early epidural infection.
