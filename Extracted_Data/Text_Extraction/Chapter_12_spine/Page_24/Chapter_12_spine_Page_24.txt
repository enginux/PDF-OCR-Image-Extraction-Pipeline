The medial-lateral position of herniation determines which nerve root will be affected.
Lesions in the central (including right or left central for lesions just parasagittal to midline) or
subarticular (lateral recess) locations may compress the escending/traversing nerve root,
while foraminal or extra-foraminal lesions may compress the exiting nerve root.
