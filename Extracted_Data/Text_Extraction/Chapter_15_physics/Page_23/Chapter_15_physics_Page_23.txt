Cavitation
Stable cavitation is regular pulsation of persistent microbubbles.
Transient cavitation is a more violent form of microbubble dynamics
characterized by large size changes in bubbles before collapse.

Tissue attenuation
Tissue attenuation is approximately 0.5 dB per cm per MHZ.
For instance, a 4 MHZ wave travelling 10 cm is attenuated 20 dB.
