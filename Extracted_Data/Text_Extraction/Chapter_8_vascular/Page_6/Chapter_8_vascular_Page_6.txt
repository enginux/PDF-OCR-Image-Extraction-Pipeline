Stanford B refers to aortic dissection that starts at the aortic arch between the
brachiocephalic trunk and left subclavian artery and not involving the ascending aorta
proximal to the brachiocephalic trunk. Since these dissections do not result in organ
ischemia, they can be treated medically.
