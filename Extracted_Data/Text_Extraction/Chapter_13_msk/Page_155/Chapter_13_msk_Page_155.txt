Paralabral cysts

Similar to the formation of parameniscal cysts adjacent to meniscal tears, paralabral cysts
form adjacent to labral tears. Paralabral cysts are usually seen in the soft tissues adjacent to
the labrum but may extend into bone. A paralabral cyst is a specific finding for a labral tear
even when abnormal labral signal is not seen.
A paralabral cyst may compress a nerve and cause an entrapment neuropathy, discussed
below.
