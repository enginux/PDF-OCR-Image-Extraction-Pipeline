Rolando fracture
Rolando fracture: PA radiograph of
the hand demonstrates a comminuted
intra-articular fracture at the base of the
thumb metacarpal (arrows).
A Rolando fracture is a comminuted Bennett fracture.
Boxer's fracture
Boxer's fracture: PA radiograph of
the hand shows a fracture of the fifth
metacarpal neck (arrow).
A Boxer's fracture is a metacarpal neck fracture, most commonly occurring in the fifth
metacarpal.
Volar plate fracture
Volar plate fracture: Lateral radiograph
shows a subtle tiny avulsion fragment
(arrow) of the volar aspect of the middle
phalanx at the PIP joint.
A volar plate fracture is an avulsion fracture of the volar aspect of the proximal middle
phalanx, caused by hyperextension.
