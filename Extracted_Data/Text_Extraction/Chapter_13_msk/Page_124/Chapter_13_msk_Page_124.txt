HIP ANATOMY
Bony acetabulum
The acetabulum is formed by the fusion of three bones: The ilium superiorly, the pubis
anteriorly, and ischium posteriorly. Pediatric radiographs of the hip show the separation of
these three bones, which meet at the triradiate cartilage to form the acetabulum.
