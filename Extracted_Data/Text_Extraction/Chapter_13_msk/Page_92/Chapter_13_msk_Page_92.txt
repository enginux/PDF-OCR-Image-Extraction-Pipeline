Osteonecrosis of the navicular is called Kohler disease in childhood and Mueller-Weiss
disease in adults. Kohler disease is typically self limited and occurs more commonly in boys,
while Mueller-Weiss disease is more severe in course and occurs more commonly in adult
women.
