Fibrous dysplasia (continued)
In the skull base, fibrous dysplasia is typically expansile and can look highly unusual on MRI.
The primary differential of an expansile skull base lesion is Paget disease, but the age of the
patient is the key: Fibrous dysplasia occurs in children and young adults and Paget occurs in
older adults.
