Parsonage-lurmer
Parsonage-Turner syndrome is idiopathic brachial neuropathy, which may cause rotator cuff
atrophy. It is a diagnosis of exclusion and a structural cause for atrophy (e.g., a mass or cyst
compressing a nerve) must be ruled out.
