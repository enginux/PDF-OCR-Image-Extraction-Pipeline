When describing a long-bone fracture, the fracture location (proximal, mid-shaft, distal),
direction of fracture, angulation, and position of the distal segment should be described.
A comminuted fracture is defined as >2 fracture fragments.
It is essential to mention if a fracture extends to the articular surface of a joint.
