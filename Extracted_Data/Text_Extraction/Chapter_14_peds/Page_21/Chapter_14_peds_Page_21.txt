Poland syndrome
Poland syndrome is an autosomal recessive syndrome of unilateral congenital absence
(complete or partial) of the pectoralis major muscle. Associated anomalies of the ipsilateral
arm and hand, including short metacarpals and syndactyly (joined fingers), may be present.
